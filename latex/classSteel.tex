\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the \mbox{\hyperlink{classSteel}{Steel}} resource in the city simulation. 

This class is a concrete product derived from \mbox{\hyperlink{classConstructionResourceProduct}{Construction\+Resource\+Product}}. It encapsulates the properties and behavior of \mbox{\hyperlink{classSteel}{Steel}} used in construction.
