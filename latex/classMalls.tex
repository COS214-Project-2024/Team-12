\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a Mall, a type of \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommercialBuilding}{Commercial\+Building}}. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classMalls}{Malls}} are initialized with a base area and optional utility connections for water, power, waste management, and sewage.
