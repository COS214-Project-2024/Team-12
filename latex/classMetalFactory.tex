\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A factory class for creating metal-\/related resources for income generation and construction. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classMetalFactory}{Metal\+Factory}} is derived from \mbox{\hyperlink{classResourceFactory}{Resource\+Factory}} and specializes in creating resources related to metals, such as gold for income and steel for construction.
