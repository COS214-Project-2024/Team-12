\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents diamonds as an income-\/generating resource in the city simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classDiamonds}{Diamonds}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classIncomeResourceProduct}{Income\+Resource\+Product}} and provides functionalities specific to diamond resources, including displaying their status and fetching their type.
