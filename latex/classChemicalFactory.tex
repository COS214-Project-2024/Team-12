\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A factory class for creating chemical resources used in construction and income generation. 

The {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classChemicalFactory}{Chemical\+Factory}}} class specializes in producing income-\/generating and construction resources. It inherits from {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classResourceFactory}{Resource\+Factory}}} and implements the factory methods to create specific types of resource products.
