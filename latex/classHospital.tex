\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a hospital that provides medical services to citizens. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classHospital}{Hospital}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classPublicService}{Public\+Service}} and includes utilities for water, electricity, sewage, and waste management.
