\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A concrete implementation of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classTransport}{Transport}} class representing a train. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTrains}{Trains}} class encapsulates the properties and behaviors of a train transport, including its capacity, speed, cost, and maintenance requirements.
