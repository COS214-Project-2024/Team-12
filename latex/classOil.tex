\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an oil resource in the simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classOil}{Oil}} class inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classIncomeResourceProduct}{Income\+Resource\+Product}} and provides functionality to manage the quantity and market value of oil resources. It allows for displaying the current status of the oil resource.
