\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a petrochemical facility that processes oil and concrete. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPetrochemicalFacility}{Petrochemical\+Facility}} class is derived from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustry}{Industry}} class and provides functionality to process oil and concrete resources, while managing the associated pollution levels and collection ranges.
