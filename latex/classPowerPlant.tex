\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a Power Plant in the utility system. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPowerPlant}{Power\+Plant}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtilityFlyweight}{Utility\+Flyweight}} and represents a power generation facility with characteristics such as cost, capacity, operational status, and the resources it consumes.

Connects the power plant and checks its operational status within a given distance.
