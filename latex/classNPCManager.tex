\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Singleton class managing the state and statistics of NPCs. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classNPCManager}{NPCManager}} class tracks various NPC states, their counts, and calculates city-\/wide metrics such as happiness level, employment, and crime rate.
