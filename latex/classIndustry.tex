\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an industrial facility in the city. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustry}{Industry}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityComponent}{City\+Component}} and is responsible for processing resources, managing pollution, and interacting with resources in the vicinity. It supports income and construction resources and integrates with a city map grid.
