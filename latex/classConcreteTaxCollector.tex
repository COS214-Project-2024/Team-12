\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A concrete implementation of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classtaxCollector}{tax\+Collector}} interface, responsible for collecting taxes from various types of buildings and zones. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classConcreteTaxCollector}{Concrete\+Tax\+Collector}} class defines specific tax collection behaviors for residential buildings, commercial buildings, and zones within the city.
